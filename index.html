Hello!

I am Simon Ross-Gill, a designer specialising in book design, page layout, illustration and interpretive maps.

Clients have included, in Scotland: NatureScot (prev. Scottish Natural Heritage), Community of Arran Seabed Trust, Woodside Arran CIC, Wee Mac Arran, Corrie's Capers books, Cruickshanks Boutique B&B, and the Whiting Bay & Districts Improvements Committee. International: Aracari Travel (Lima, Peru), Horizon Guides (Sheffield, England), White Tiger PR (London, England), West Cork Fit (Ireland), Kalpak Travel (Switzerland) Travel Without Plastic (Canary Isles, Spain), Group-Q (Denver, U.S.A.) and Infinite Safari Adventures (Los Angeles, U.S.A.).

I am committed to producing high calibre and functional graphic design using open source / subscription free software wherever possible. Transparent communication with my clients is a priority and working together to find a sustainable solution for you and your business or organisation. Please contact me to discuss working together or to request a quote.

The Isle of Arran

I'm based on the Isle of Arran in Scotland, where I grew up. The natural beauty of Arran is a constant inspiration for my creative work and I am dedicated to supporting sustainability, conservation and regeneration of our natural environment. In 2019 I set up The Arran Green Map to showcase the diversity of local projects and initiatives working towards this.
