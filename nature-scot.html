Nature.Scot Community-led Marine Biodiversity Monitoring in Scotland

In 2020 I completed the design of The Community-led Marine Biodiversity Monitoring Handbook for Nature.Scot (previously Scottish Natural Heritage), Scotland's nature agency. This detailed handbook includes over 140 pages of full spread colour photographs, diagrams, tables, forms and other details. The design was completed for print and will be distributed to coastal communities around Scotland enabling local people to undertake surveys of biodiversity in Scotland's waters.
